Adam Pattberg

My name is Adam Pattberg and I am 21 years old. I am from Minnesota, but currently pursuing a Bachelors of Advaced Vehicle Systems at Brigham Young University - Idaho. I like anything with an engine and understanding how and why it works. I love exploring natural phenomemons, learning about history, and learning new things. I used to work in my high schools technical theater department and worked as an EMT. My goal career is in vehicle testing and validation.

Vehicles

I love anything with an engine in it. Cars are the easiest expression of this, but I also love planes, helicopters, motorcycles, jetskis, boats, etc. I had the privilege of flying a Cessna 172 skyhawk several years ago, which was an amazing experience. It is on my bucket list to fly a helicopter one day.

Theater & Film

I was a part of my middle and high schools theater program, but only on the technical side. Through the countless hours I spent there, I learned many different skills and experiences. One thing I learned was how to appreciate the art of theater and film. I have since come to love movies and shows. There are many films are on my list that I have not seen yet, and unfortunately, the list gets longer every day.

Natures Beauty

During my time in Boy Scouts, I came to love the outdoors. I love to learn about and experience the many natural wonders of the world. I love understanding how these features formed. For example, there is a national preserve called Craters of the Moon. This is an area in Idaho that had lava flow across it that then melted into the ground, creating some amazing caves and tunnel systems.
